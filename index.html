DESIGN DETAILS

Detachable Design  可拆分设计

The mask is divided into three parts connected by magnetic force. When the user does not need to cover the face, he/she can remove the upper and lower parts and use it as a sunglasses.
面罩分为三个部分，通过磁力连接。当使用者不需要遮挡脸部时，可移除上下两部分后作为墨镜使用。 

Detailed Dimension  详细尺寸

In the design details of the mask, we measured the sizes of the heads of the young men and women, and obtained a reasonable proportion of the long heads. then we dimensioned the mask. the unit here is millimeter.
在面罩的设计细节中，我们测量了男女青年头部的各项尺寸，通过分析得到较为合理的长款高比例。我们对面罩进行了尺寸标注。这里的单位是毫米。

Multiple Styles  多种款式

According to different people's face shape and different preferences, we made a different style design in the glasses part of the mask, and brought more choices to consumers.
根据不同人的脸型以及不同的喜好，我们在面罩的眼镜部分做出了不一样的款式设计，给消费者们带来了更多的选择。
